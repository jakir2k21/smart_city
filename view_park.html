S P Park - এসপি পার্ক

At the west end of Tangail city, the attractive SP Park has been built on both sides of the canal adjacent to Laujong River and Police Lines after restoration and excavation. Starting from Policeline High School to District Jail, the main gate of the park has been constructed at Hazra Ghat. Rows of colorful flowering trees have been planted on both sides of the canal. There are different rides for children at different ends of the field.
